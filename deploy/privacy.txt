PRIVACY POLICY FOR MEDDEUTSCH

Last Updated: January 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

MedDeutsch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information
- Email address (for account creation)
- Name (optional, for personalization)
- Learning progress and preferences

2.2 Usage Data
- App usage statistics
- Learning progress
- Quiz scores and completion rates
- Device information (type, OS version)

2.3 Authentication Data
- Login credentials (encrypted)
- Third-party authentication tokens (Google, Apple Sign-In)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Track your learning progress
- Personalize your learning experience
- Send notifications about your progress
- Improve our app and content
- Process subscription payments
- Provide customer support

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- All personal data is stored securely using Firebase services
- Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:
- Firebase (Authentication, Database, Analytics)
- Google Sign-In
- Apple Sign-In
- RevenueCat (Subscription management)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

10. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

11. CONSENT

By using MedDeutsch, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
