## 6. Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (Next Release)

1. **Add Video Content Integration**
   - Link to YouTube videos or embed video lessons
   - Add video demonstrations for Anamnese, Case Presentation

2. **Enhance FSP Simulation**
   - Full timed exam simulation
   - All three FSP components (Anamnese, Documentation, Doctor-Doctor)

3. **Add Writing Practice**
   - Arztbrief templates with guided writing
   - Sample discharge letters with explanation

## 8. Conclusion

The MedDeutsch app provides **solid foundational content** covering all essential medical German topics for FSP and KP preparation. The main gaps are:

1. **Video content** - The most significant missing feature
2. **Interactive simulations** - Real-time practice with feedback
3. **Writing exercises** - Guided documentation practice
4. **Live coaching** - Personalized instruction

The app's strengths in **offline access** and **multi-language support** differentiate it from the website offering. Prioritizing video integration and enhanced FSP simulation would significantly improve the app's competitiveness.
